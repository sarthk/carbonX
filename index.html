Why It Matters

🏳️

Air Quality Crisis

14 of the world's 20 most polluted cities are in India. Delhi's AQI regularly crosses 400. The air you breathe is directly linked to fossil fuel emissions from transport, industry, and power generation.

🌡️

Climate Impact on India

India faces extreme heatwaves exceeding 50°C, erratic monsoons causing both floods and droughts, and rising sea levels threatening coastal cities. By 2050, climate change could cost India 2.8% of GDP annually.

✊

Individuals Can Make a Difference

If every Indian household switched to LED lights, we'd save 40 billion kWh/year. Choosing public transport over a car for 10 km daily saves 0.8 tonnes CO2/year. Small changes, multiplied by 1.4 billion, are massive.
